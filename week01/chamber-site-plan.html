Overview

Planned Site Name: Maricopa Commerce Chamber

The name represents Arizona's Maricopa County, which spans Pheonix and its immediate surrounding cities.



Site Purpose

Maricopa Commerce Chamber aims to promote communication and collaboration as well as increasing economic groowth potential between community members and businesses through community event hosting, Development concernng business prospects, and through individual leadership training.

Scenarios

Maricopa Commerce Chamber provides monthly events themed around networking, surveys, members and board meetings

Maricopa Commerce Chamber also seeks to offer leadership and other low cost basic business training at for individuals through montly courses. This gives us an oppurtunity to help individuals within our community regardless of whether they are employed with our members or not.
